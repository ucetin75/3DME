3DME — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

3DME processes your photos and videos exclusively on your Apple TV. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Photo Access

3DME requires access to your iCloud Photo Library to display and convert photos and videos to 3D. All processing (AI depth estimation, 3D rendering) happens entirely on the device.

Stored Data

Only your personal settings (3D intensity, language, display format) are stored locally on the device using standard system preferences. No photos, videos, or personal data are stored by the app.

No Data Transmission

3DME does not send any photos, videos, or personal data to servers. The app contains no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

In-App Purchase

The optional purchase is processed through Apple's App Store. 3DME has no access to your payment information. All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple.

Shared Albums

Photos from shared iCloud albums have their depth data removed by Apple during sharing. 3DME uses AI depth estimation for these photos.

Children's Privacy

3DME does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
